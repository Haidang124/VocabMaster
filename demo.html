🖍️ English Vocabulary Extension Demo

📋 Hướng dẫn sử dụng:

  1. Double-click vào từ bất kỳ trong đoạn văn bên dưới
  2. Nhấn Alt+H để highlight và lưu từ
  3. Mở extension popup để xem danh sách từ đã lưu
  4. Sử dụng tab "Từ Ngẫu Nhiên" để ôn tập

🎯 Thử highlight các từ này:

The magnificent mountain range stretched across the horizon, its peaks covered in pristine snow. The landscape was truly breathtaking, with crystal-clear streams flowing through lush valleys. This extraordinary wilderness provided a perfect sanctuary for wildlife and adventurers alike.

📖 Đoạn văn mẫu để thực hành:

Technology has revolutionized the way we communicate and interact with the world. The internet has made information accessible to billions of people, transforming education, business, and entertainment. Artificial intelligence is becoming increasingly sophisticated, offering new possibilities for automation and innovation.

Climate change represents one of the most pressing challenges of our time. Rising temperatures, melting ice caps, and extreme weather events are evidence of this global crisis. Sustainable practices and renewable energy sources are essential for addressing these environmental concerns.

💡 Gợi ý từ để highlight:

  • magnificent, pristine, breathtaking
  • revolutionized, sophisticated, innovation
  • pressing, sustainable, renewable
  • extraordinary, wilderness, sanctuary
  • accessible, transforming, artificial

🔧 Cấu hình Google Sheets:

Để tự động lưu từ mới vào Google Sheets:

  1. Tạo Google Sheets mới
  2. Copy URL của sheet
  3. Mở extension popup
  4. Paste URL vào ô "Google Sheets URL"
  5. Click "Lưu URL Sheet"
